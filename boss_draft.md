### 2. From RWA to RWS: An Upgrade in Definition, Not Just a New Narrative

**What is RWS (Real-World Solution)?**
- RWS is a broader and more fundamental concept.
- In a sense:
  - Bitcoin
  - Ethereum
  - Blockchain itself
  - are all real-world solutions.

**Why "RWS today" does NOT simply mean "on-chain payments everywhere"?**

The real world has real friction:
- Existing payment habits
- User onboarding barriers
- Asset security concerns
- Regulatory and compliance costs

So:
- "Everything on-chain payments" is more like the endgame
- At the current stage, RWS should evolve:
  - From decentralized finance
  - To decentralized real-world services

**One-sentence Summary**
RWS = embedding blockchain's value-creation capability into real-world services and interactions.
